Project Overview
This document outlines the scope and features of a billing software built specifically for your camera shop. The goal is to make day-to-day billing, product management, and customer tracking simple and efficient — all from a single web application.
Project Type
Web Application (accessible from browser on any device)
Access
Single admin login — the shop owner or any user with the credentials can log in and manage the system
Platform
Works on desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile browser — fully mobile responsive

Module 4 — Billing & Invoice
This is the main working page of the software. You can create new bills, view past bills, generate a PDF invoice, and share it directly on WhatsApp — all from the same page.
4.1  Creating a New Bill
Select the customer from your saved list (or add a new one on the spot)
Search and add products to the bill
Set quantity for each item — price calculates automatically
Apply GST if applicable (optional toggle per bill)
Enter GST percentage (e.g. 5%, 12%, 18%)
System auto-calculates subtotal and final total
Mark the bill as Paid or Unpaid
⚠  Pending Client Decision
How should GST be applied to the product price?

Option A — Inclusive: GST is already included within the product price.
Example: Product price = ₹10,000. This already includes 18% GST. The GST amount shown on the invoice = ₹1,525, and the base price = ₹8,475.

Option B — Exclusive: GST is added on top of the product price.
Example: Product price = ₹10,000. GST 18% is added separately. Final invoice total = ₹11,800.

Please confirm which method your shop follows.
4.2  Managing Bills
View list of all past bills
Filter bills by date, customer, or payment status (Paid / Unpaid)
Open and view any past bill
Edit a bill before it is finalised
4.3  Invoice PDF & WhatsApp Sharing
Generate a clean, professional PDF invoice for any bill
Invoice includes: shop name, customer details, itemised product list, GST breakdown (if applied), and total amount
Share the invoice directly to the customer via WhatsApp with one click
